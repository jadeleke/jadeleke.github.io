About Joseph Adeleke, MSc

"To be, is to do." — Versatile engineer specializing in Multi-Cloud DevSecOps and MLOps. Focuses on secure, scalable cloud architectures and AI-driven workflows, with a strong enthusiasm for automation and bridging the gap between development and operations.

What I bring

  • Model operations expertise spanning experiment tracking, automated retraining, and safe rollout strategies — with hands-on experience building CNN classifiers, Whisper ASR systems, and Wav2Vec2 transcription pipelines.
  • Proven delivery of cloud-native infrastructure, CI/CD (GitHub Actions & Azure DevOps Pipelines), and observability tailored to data science workloads.
  • Deep Oracle DBA and Linux background for hybrid environments where legacy and modern platforms converge.
  • Strong containerisation skills with Docker, Docker Swarm, and Kubernetes for production ML deployments.

Quick facts

  • MSc Computer Science from the University of Ghana, with research focus on speech classification for Ghanaian languages.
  • Hands-on with Kubernetes, MLflow, Terraform, Docker, and Azure DevOps Pipelines.
  • Comfortable across Azure, Oracle Database, and hardened Linux estates.
  • Passionate about African NLP and audio transcription (GhanaVoice, Akan-Whisper, Multi-Language-Classifier).
